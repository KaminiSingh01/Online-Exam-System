Digiexam: Online Assessment Software for Companies & Organizations

Digiexam is a powerful yet easy-to-use online assessment platform designed for businesses and organizations. Whether you're managing professional training, conducting course examinations, or running recruitment screenings, Digiexam simplifies the entire process.

Why Choose Digiexam for Your Online Tests?

Intuitive & Easy to Use

Digiexam is designed for simplicity and efficiency, allowing you to get started in minutes. The platform offers all the powerful features you need without the hassle of complex integrations, extensive training, or software downloads. Its clean, user-friendly interface makes creating, managing, and distributing exams effortless—even for first-time users.

Secure & Reliable

Digiexam ensures test integrity with its high-security mode. Test takers are locked into full-screen mode, preventing them from switching tabs or accessing unauthorized content. Real-time monitoring gives you full visibility and control over participant behavior.

Time-Efficient Grading & Feedback

Save time with auto-marked tests. The platform automatically grades multiple-choice and short-answer questions according to the rules you set. With just a few clicks, you can distribute test results to the entire group, making the process fast and seamless.
