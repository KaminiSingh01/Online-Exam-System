Your Privacy is Our Priority

At DigiExam, we prioritize the security and privacy of your data. Used globally in over 100 countries, we comply with regional privacy laws such as GDPR, FERPA, COPPA, and SOPIPA, ensuring your data is safe and handled responsibly.

A Message from Our Team

Created by a passionate team of former educators, DigiExam is committed to safeguarding your privacy:

  • Data Collection: We collect personal data only to provide our services and connect exams to the right students.
  • Ownership: You own all the exams, quizzes, and questions.
  • No Ads: We do not show ads on our platform.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We will never sell or share your data with third parties.
  • Student Data: We process student data solely for exam-related purposes, based on your settings.

Compliance with Privacy Regulations

DigiExam is fully compliant with:

  • GDPR: We adhere to EU data protection laws.
  • FERPA: We protect U.S. student records.
  • COPPA: We protect children’s data under 13.
  • SOPIPA: We secure student data in California.
