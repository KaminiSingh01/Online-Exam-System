Digiexam: Online Assessment Software for Schools

Digiexam is an online assessment platform that empowers teachers and school staff to create, conduct, and grade both formative and summative exams. Supporting educators is at the core of our mission, and we help thousands of schools streamline their assessment processes every day.

Why Digiexam is Your Solution for Online Exams

Intuitive & Easy to Use

Digiexam is designed with simplicity in mind, allowing you to create and manage exams effortlessly. The platform offers a seamless user experience with a clean interface, enabling teachers to get started in minutes—no training, integrations, or software downloads required.

Time-Efficient Grading & Feedback

Save valuable time with auto-marked exams. The platform automatically grades multiple-choice and short-answer questions based on your preset rules. For open-ended questions, you can provide feedback directly within the platform. With just a few clicks, you can distribute exam results and personalized feedback to the entire class.

Robust Cheat Prevention & Security

Ensure academic integrity with Digiexam’s advanced security features. The platform offers multiple security modes, including full-screen lockdown, browser restrictions, and plagiarism detection. These measures help prevent cheating and maintain the credibility of both low-stakes practice tests and high-stakes final exams.

Flexible Accommodations & Accessibility

Digiexam allows you to customize exams to meet the diverse needs of your students. Built-in accommodations include text-to-speech functionality, translation options, and the ability to grant extra time for students who need it. This flexibility ensures an inclusive and fair testing environment.
